About Us

We are awesome TEAM


Borno Technology Forum is an initiative aimed at bringing together technology enthusiast in order rub minds and engage in coordinated activities centred around using technology to propel rapid development in Borno State, North- Eastern Nigeria and the lake Chad region in general. The need for such an initiative is important given that Borno state is just coming out of a major crisis that has pulled it several years backwards developmentally. Technology, when applied properly is arguably not just the quickest means of making up for the economic loss sufered but also a sure tool for building a future ready economy in Borno state and the Northeast.
An economy that will be a model not only in Nigeria but also in Africa and the world at large.

Objectives

Bring together Technology enthusiast in Borno to network and exchange ideas..

Promote the general development of a technology ecosystem in Borno state and the North-East..

Persistently persuade the government to be deploying modern technology to improve its activities.

Partner with the government and other non-governmental organizations towards the establishment and sustenance of technology innovation hubs in Borno and North-Eastern Nigeria..

Encourage members to improve one another by sharing knowledge and experiences.

  • Bring together Technology enthusiast in Borno to network and exchange ideas.
  • Act as a think-tank on issues relating to use of technology for development.
  • Encourage members to improve one another by sharing knowledge and experiences.
  • Bridge the gap between the local technology based businesses and the academics.
  • Encourage people in Borno - especially the youth - to start technology based ventures..
  • Build bridges with other technology ecosystems across Nigeria and around the world..
  • Guide public and private organizations on best ways to use technology to improve their activities..
  • Use technology to fght youth unemployment and underemployment.
  • Help attract grants and investments to technology ventures that may crop up as a result of the forum's activities..
